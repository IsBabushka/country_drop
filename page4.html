Who we are

Our Mission

To ensure that every drop of water you drink is safe, healthy, and refreshing.

Our Story

Country Drop Water Refilling Station was born out of resilience and a desire to serve the community with pure and clean water. The journey began in late 2022 when Rodel Catanghal returned to the Philippines after the pandemic. Faced with a challenging job market and uncertainty about the future, Romel and his family saw an opportunity to make a difference.

Recognizing the growing demand for safe and reliable drinking water, the Catanghal family decided to establish a water refilling station. With hard work and dedication, they transformed their vision into reality. Today, Country Drop is proudly run by the Catanghal family, with the father, Rodel Catanghal, and mother, Cristina Catanghal at the heart of operations, ensuring every drop meets the highest standards of purity and cleanliness.

Located in Bacoor, Cavite, Country Drop has quickly become a trusted name in the community. Our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction reflects the values of the Catanghal family, who strive to provide not just water, but peace of mind to every household and business we serve.
